About Me

I have almost 10 years of experience in evaluating and extracting different types of data and translating raw information into reports and presentations that companies can use to support their decisions.

I started my career as a General Accountant, and most recently, I worked as a Finance Supervisor at Pawnhero Pawnshop Philippines, serving in various capacities as a Team Leader and Financial Reporting Manager.

Recently, I participated in Eskwelabs’ Data Analyst Bootcamp, where I applied concepts to solve real-world problems. For my capstone project, I collaborated with a small group to build a segmentation analysis for a fintech app to aid the operations team in enhancing user engagement. In that project, I used tools such as SQL and GDS.

Currently, I work full-time as a data analyst.
